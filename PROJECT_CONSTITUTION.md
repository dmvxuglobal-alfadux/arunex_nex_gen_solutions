# Arunex Gen Solutions - Project Constitution

## PROJECT CONTEXT
This project is to design and develop the official website for **Arunex Gen Solutions**, an **AI-Powered Business Transformation Company**. The website strictly follows the implementation blueprint established in Master Prompts 1 through 8.

All architectural decisions, design components, and engineering standards are bound by this document. Do not reinterpret, expand, duplicate, or replace any requirements unless explicitly instructed.

---

## PROJECT OBJECTIVE
The objective is to create a premium enterprise website that communicates Arunex's expertise in AI, Automation, Analytics, Cloud, and Digital Transformation while maintaining a clean, executive, business-first experience.

The website must feel modern, trustworthy, and focused on helping business leaders understand Arunex's solutions and confidently book an AI Strategy Session.

---

## IMPLEMENTATION RULES

### Design System
- Use **only** the design language defined in Master Prompt 1 (Midnight Sapphire palette, Space Grotesk + Inter typography).
- Do not introduce additional color palettes, typography systems, spacing rules, or visual styles.

### Page Structure
Build only the following approved pages:
- Home (`/`)
- Solutions (`/solutions`)
- AI Business Starter (`/solutions/starter`)
- AI Growth Accelerator (`/solutions/growth`)
- Digital Enterprise (`/solutions/enterprise`)
- About (`/about`)
- Industries (`/industries` - embedded in About)
- Innovation Ecosystem (`/ecosystem` - embedded in About)
- Contact (`/contact`)

Do not create additional marketing pages unless explicitly requested.

### Solution Pages
- Maintain **one reusable layout** for AI Business Starter, AI Growth Accelerator, and Digital Enterprise.
- Only the business content changes. Do not redesign each page independently.

### Components
- Reuse components across the entire website.
- Avoid duplicate UI. Maintain a single, cohesive design system.

### Motion
- Implement only the motion system defined in Master Prompt 7.
- Animations must remain subtle, executive, and performance-friendly.
- Avoid unnecessary animation experiments.

### Development
- Follow the engineering standards defined in Master Prompt 8 (Next.js App Router, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript).
- Keep the architecture clean, modular, scalable, and production-ready.

### Content
- Content must remain concise, executive, and business-focused.
- Avoid unnecessary marketing buzzwords, long paragraphs, or repeated information. Communicate business value with absolute clarity.

### User Experience
Guide visitors through a simple, high-converting journey:
**Business Problem → Arunex Solution → Business Outcomes → Book an AI Strategy Session**

### Performance
Prioritize:
- Performance
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA)
- SEO
- Responsiveness
- Maintainability

Every implementation decision must support these priorities.

### Design Consistency
Every page must maintain:
- Same spacing system
- Same typography
- Same card design
- Same button styles
- Same navigation
- Same footer
- Same animations
- Same interaction language

The website must feel like one unified product.

### Scope Control
- Implement **only** what is defined in Master Prompts 1–8.
- Do not invent additional sections, features, dashboards, widgets, pages, or functionality beyond the approved scope.
- When multiple implementation options exist, always choose the simplest, cleanest, and most maintainable solution.

---

## FINAL GOAL
Deliver a premium, production-ready website for Arunex Gen Solutions that faithfully follows the approved design system, page architecture, motion language, and engineering standards. The final product is elegant, consistent, scalable, SEO-friendly, accessible, high-performance, and ruthlessly focused on converting visitors into AI Strategy Session bookings.
